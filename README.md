## What is ChackTok?

ChackTok is a **commercial-grade software platform** that enables professional photo booth operators to capture, process, and deliver branded event media at scale, with comprehensive support for **360-degree video booths** and **iPad-based photo booth setups**.

Built for high-frequency commercial operations, ChackTok provides:

- Automated event workflow management
- Multi-brand hardware integration and control
- Professional content customization and branding
- Efficient guest sharing and delivery systems
- Scalable business operations across multiple devices and locations

## Core Capabilities

### Event Workflow Automation

- **Template-based setup:** Create events in under 3 minutes using reusable templates
- **Multi-event management:** Track and manage multiple concurrent events
- **Event modes:** Sharing Station, Auto Station, Party Mode for different operational needs
- **Password protection:** Lock event settings to prevent accidental changes

### Multi-Hardware Integration

- **12+ booth brands supported:** MWE, OLYLO, YCKJNB, ZANOGI, FUTOBOOZ, HARZHI, ZLPOWER, DIANDIAN, LIKVIDIU, JIYANG, KRICIKR, and more
- **Bluetooth control:** Direct device control for rotation speed, direction, and duration
- **GoPro integration:** Full support for GoPro HERO 9/10/11/12/13 (wired and wireless)
- **Motion triggering:** Automated capture when booth rotation is detected
- **Peripheral support:** WiFi printers, external displays, remote controls

### Branded Content Output

- **Custom overlays:** Frames, stickers, logos, and text overlays
- **Video effects:** Filters, beauty modes, stabilization, and custom video modes
- **Background music:** Upload custom audio tracks or select from library
- **Preroll & Postroll:** Branded intro and outro video clips
- **Photo templates:** Fully customizable photo layouts and backgrounds

### Guest Sharing & Delivery

- **QR code sharing:** Instant guest access without app installation
- **SMS/Email delivery:** Direct media delivery to guests (where supported)
- **Social platform integration:** Direct sharing to social media
- **Gallery hosting:** Cloud-based event galleries with public URLs
- **Scan & Shoot:** QR-triggered capture for self-service operations

### Business Management

- **Web console:** PC-based management at [https://pc.chacktok.com](https://pc.chacktok.com/)
- **Media library:** Centralized storage and batch operations
- **Multi-device login:** Sync settings across multiple devices
- **Event history:** Complete archive of past events and content

## Why Operators Choose ChackTok

Photo booth operators switch to — and stay with — ChackTok for five core reasons:

### 1. Hardware Freedom

Most photo booth software locks you to a single hardware vendor. ChackTok supports **12+ booth brands** and **GoPro HERO 9–13**, giving operators the freedom to buy the best-value hardware from any manufacturer. Mixed fleets can save **20–40% on hardware costs** compared to vendor-locked alternatives.

### 2. Operational Speed

Template-based event setup takes **under 3 minutes** — vs. 15–20 minutes of manual configuration with generic apps. Clone, customize, and deploy. Settings sync across devices so any operator can run any booth.

### 3. Guest Experience

QR code sharing with **no app download required**. Guests scan → view → share to social media in seconds. This eliminates the #1 source of client complaints in the industry: slow or complicated content delivery.

### 4. Business Scale

One account manages multiple devices, multiple events, and multiple operators. The **PC web console** ([pc.chacktok.com](http://pc.chacktok.com/)) provides centralized management. Whether you run 1 booth or 10+, the workflow stays the same.

### 5. Professional Output

Custom overlays, branded preroll/postroll, beauty filters, slow motion, and background music — all configurable per event. Output quality meets **corporate and wedding professional standards**, commanding premium pricing from clients.

1. **Download ChackTok**
   - iOS: [App Store](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/chacktok-photo-booth-event/id1613120226)

4. **Connect Hardware**

   - Start event
   - Test full workflow
   - Share first result
