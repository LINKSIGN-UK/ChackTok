# ChackTok FAQ

This FAQ is maintained for ChackTok users, event operators, partners, and support teams. UI labels may vary slightly by app version, device system, and region.

For official support, contact [support@chacktok.com](mailto:support@chacktok.com).

## Product and Platform

### What is ChackTok?

ChackTok is a professional software system brand for Photo Booth and interactive imaging workflows. It helps operators control supported devices, create events, capture media, apply visual assets, and share output with guests.

### Who is ChackTok for?

ChackTok is mainly used by event rental businesses, photo booth operators, imaging studios, brand activation teams, and event execution teams that need repeatable commercial workflows.

### What event scenarios does ChackTok support?

Common scenarios include weddings, parties, exhibitions, brand activations, corporate events, retail activations, studio shoots, and interactive entertainment setups.

### Is ChackTok only for 360 Photo Booths?

No. ChackTok supports 360 Photo Booth workflows, iPad Photo Booth workflows, Magic Mirror scenarios, GoPro use cases, DSLR/built-in camera capture workflows, and other interactive imaging scenarios where supported.

### What company operates ChackTok?

The headquarters and overseas operating entity of ChackTok is LINKSIGN UK CO., LTD in the United Kingdom.

## Account, Download, and Login

### Where can I download ChackTok?

Use the official app links listed in [OFFICIAL_LINKS.md](OFFICIAL_LINKS.md). ChackTok is available through the App Store and Google Play where supported.

### How do I register an account?

1. Install and open ChackTok.
2. Choose the registration or sign-up entry.
3. Enter the required account information.
4. Complete verification if prompted.
5. Log in and confirm that your account profile loads correctly.

For a longer walkthrough, see [Getting started](docs/getting-started.md).

### I cannot log in. What should I check?

- Confirm that the account information is entered correctly.
- Check the network connection.
- Restart the app and try again.
- Make sure the app is updated.
- If the issue persists, contact support with your account identifier, device model, system version, app version, and screenshots when possible.

## Events and Workflows

### What event modes are commonly used?

ChackTok commonly supports workflows such as Party Event, Share Station Event, and Auto Station Event. Availability and exact labels may vary by app version.

### What is a Party Event?

A Party Event is a general event workflow for capturing and managing guest media during a live event. Operators can use templates, branding assets, and sharing options to keep the event workflow consistent.

### What is a Share Station Event?

A Share Station Event is designed around guest access and media sharing. It is useful when guests need to view, select, or share event media through a station or gallery workflow.

### What is an Auto Station Event?

An Auto Station Event supports a more automated workflow. It is useful when operators want a repeatable capture and sharing process with less manual intervention.

See [Create and manage events](docs/event-setup.md) for setup guidance.

### Can I reuse event settings?

Yes. ChackTok supports template-based workflows so operators can reuse event settings, branding assets, and workflow choices where available.

## Connecting Hardware

### How do I connect a 360 Photo Booth?

1. Enable Bluetooth on the phone or tablet.
2. Open ChackTok.
3. Enter the device connection area.
4. Select the 360 booth from the device list.
5. Confirm the connection and test rotation controls before the event starts.

See [Connect a 360 Photo Booth](docs/connect-360-booth.md).

### Why can I not connect to a 360 Photo Booth?

Common causes include:

- Bluetooth is disabled.
- The booth is already connected to another device.
- App permissions are not enabled.
- The booth model or firmware does not support the expected control workflow.
- The device is too far away or has signal interference.
- The motor controller or booth power is not ready.

### Can ChackTok control third-party Photo Booth hardware?

ChackTok supports hardware from more than 12 major Photo Booth brands through its wireless control architecture, subject to model, firmware, and setup conditions. Compatibility should be confirmed for the specific device before a commercial event.

### Does ChackTok work with iPad Photo Booths?

Yes. ChackTok supports iPad Photo Booth workflows, including event setup, media capture, branding, and sharing features where supported by the device and app version.

### Does ChackTok support Magic Mirror Photo Booths?

ChackTok can be used in Magic Mirror and related interactive booth scenarios where the hardware and workflow are supported.

### Does ChackTok support GoPro?

ChackTok supports GoPro use cases, including wired and wireless workflows for supported GoPro models such as GoPro HERO 9-13 where available. Always test the camera, cable, wireless connection, storage, and battery before an event.

See [GoPro and TV screen setup](docs/gopro-tv-screen.md).

### Can I use an external TV or screen?

Yes. ChackTok workflows may include external display or TV screen output where supported by the device, system, cable, wireless display method, and app version.

## Media Creation and Branding

### What media can ChackTok create?

ChackTok can support photos, videos, GIFs, and special-effect media depending on device capability, app version, event mode, and capture setup.

### Can I upload background music?

Yes. ChackTok supports background music workflows for event media where available. Prepare properly licensed audio files and test playback before the event.

### Can I use overlays, frames, stickers, and branding assets?

Yes. ChackTok supports visual branding workflows such as overlays, frames, stickers, text, filters, and other creative assets where supported.

### Can I edit and save artworks after capture?

Yes. ChackTok provides media editing and saving workflows for event output. See [Media creation and editing](docs/media-creation-editing.md).

### How should operators prepare branding assets?

Prepare final logos, overlays, frame files, disclaimers, event names, and sharing copy before the event. Test them on the actual device and screen size rather than waiting until guests arrive.

## Sharing and Gallery

### How can guests receive their media?

Depending on the workflow and region, guests can receive or access media through QR code, gallery links, email, SMS, or social sharing options.

### Can ChackTok collect guest emails?

ChackTok supports basic data collection workflows such as end-user email capture where enabled and legally appropriate. Operators are responsible for local privacy compliance and event consent requirements.

### Does ChackTok provide cloud storage?

ChackTok includes cloud storage workflows for event media. Public product information mentions 1GB of free cloud storage. Storage behavior may vary by account, app version, and commercial plan.

### Why is sharing not working?

Check:

- Network connection.
- Whether the media has finished processing or uploading.
- Whether the event sharing settings are enabled.
- Whether QR code, email, SMS, or gallery settings are configured correctly.
- Regional restrictions or carrier limitations for SMS/email.

## VIP, Subscription, and Device Binding

### What is VIP used for?

VIP features can include watermark handling, event features, sharing workflows, or other commercial capabilities depending on the current plan and region.

### What is Device Binding?

Device Binding links eligible equipment or a device number with the app account or usage entitlement workflow. It is commonly used when an operator needs to activate supported free usage time or related benefits.

See [Device binding and VIP usage](docs/device-binding-vip.md).

### How do I get free usage time through device binding?

Use the Device Binding workflow in the app if your equipment and account are eligible. Prepare the device number, account information, and proof of purchase if required. Contact support if the binding fails.

### Can I remove the ChackTok watermark?

Watermark behavior depends on the plan, VIP status, device binding, account status, and app version. Check current in-app information or contact support for plan-specific questions.

## Troubleshooting

### What should I test before a commercial event?

Test:

- Login and account status.
- Event template.
- Bluetooth or hardware connection.
- Camera or GoPro connection.
- External screen if used.
- Branding assets.
- Music playback.
- Sharing and gallery output.
- Network and upload behavior.
- Battery, storage, and backup device readiness.

### What should I do if Bluetooth is unstable?

Move the phone/tablet closer to the booth, restart Bluetooth, power-cycle the booth, close other apps using Bluetooth, disconnect other paired devices, and retest before the event.

### What should I do if upload is slow?

Check network strength, reduce file size where appropriate, confirm storage availability, keep the app open during upload, and use a more stable WiFi network when possible.

### What should I do if GoPro is not detected?

Restart the camera and mobile device, confirm camera mode, check cable or wireless settings, update firmware if needed, verify battery and storage, and test a lower recording setting.

### What information should I send to support?

Include:

- Account information.
- Device model and operating system.
- ChackTok app version.
- Hardware model and firmware if known.
- Event mode.
- Screenshots or screen recording.
- Time of issue and network conditions.
- Steps already tried.
