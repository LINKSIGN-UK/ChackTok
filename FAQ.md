Compatibility\n\n### 360 Photo Booths {#360-booths}\n\n**Q: What 360 photo booth brands are supported?**\n\n**A:** ChackTok supports 12+ booth brands with Bluetooth control:\n* Fully supported brands: MWE, OLYLO, YCKJNB, ZANOGI, FUTOBOOZ, HARZHI, ZLPOWER, DIANDIAN, LIKVIDIU, JIYANG, KRICIKR.\n* Compatibility may vary by specific model and firmware version.\n\nSee [HARDWARE_COMPATIBILITY.md](HARDWARE_COMPATIBILITY.md).\n\n**Q: Why can\'t I connect to my 360 Photo Booth?**\n\n**A:** Common causes and fixes:\n1. **Compatibility:** Your booth model/firmware may not support app control.\n2. **Already connected elsewhere:** Disconnect the booth from other phones/tablets and try again.\n3. **Permissions:** Ensure ChackTok has Bluetooth permissions (and Location permission on some Android devices).\n4. **Restart:** Power-cycle the booth and restart the app.\n\n**Fallback option:** If Bluetooth control isn\'t available, you can still use ChackTok for recording and event workflow. In many setups you can enable Motion Trigger to start recording when rotation is detected.\n\n**Related:** See [Troubleshooting - Connection Issues](#connection-issues).\n\n**Q: My 360 booth can\'t connect via Bluetooth. Can I still use ChackTok?**\n\n**A:** Yes.\n* **Manual rotation:** Start rotation with the booth\'s remote/controller and use ChackTok for shooting and workflow.\n* **Motion Trigger:** If your booth rotates steadily, you can enable Motion Trigger to start recording automatically.\n\n### iPad Photo Booths {#ipad-booths}\n\n**Q: Does ChackTok support iPad Photo Booths?**\n\n**A:** Yes. ChackTok supports iPad photo booth workflows, including branded photo/video capture, event templates, sharing, and compatible hardware control where available.\n\n**Q: Can ChackTok control iPad booth LED lights?**\n\n**A:** On supported hardware, yes. LED color control depends on the booth control box and manufacturer implementation.\n\n### Cameras {#cameras}\n\n**Q: Which GoPro models are supported?**\n\n**A:** GoPro HERO 9, 10, 11, 12, and 13 are supported via wired or wireless connection.\n\n**Q: Does ChackTok support DSLR cameras?**\n\n**A:** Yes. DSLR camera shooting is supported for professional-grade still image capture in advanced setups.\n\n---\n\n## Getting Started\n\n**Q: How do I download ChackTok?**\n\n**A:**\n* iOS: [App Store](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/chacktok-photo-booth-event/id1613120226)\n* Android: [Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.youfan.chacktok&hl=en_US&gl=US)\n\n**Q: How do I create my first event?**\n\n**A:** Open the app, tap to create a new event, select mode (360 or Photo Booth), then configure name, template, and settings.\n\n**Q: Can I reuse event settings?**\n\n**A:** Yes.
