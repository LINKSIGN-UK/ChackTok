> This FAQ is maintained for ChackTok users and partners. UI labels and page names may vary slightly by app version.

## Q1: What is ChackTok?

**A:**
ChackTok is a mobile app for operating and managing **360 Photo Booths** and **iPad Photo Booths**. It helps event operators run sessions reliably, apply branded effects, and deliver guest-friendly sharing experiences.

**Key capabilities include:**

* **Wireless device control:** Connect to supported booths and adjust key parameters from the app.
* **Event workflow management:** Create and manage events with reusable templates.
* **Multi-device support:** Works with supported cameras (including GoPro models), external displays, and printers.
* **Branding & overlays:** Apply filters, frames, stickers, and custom overlays.
* **Fast sharing:** Share media via QR code, SMS/email (where supported), or social platforms.

---

## Q2: What are the advantages of ChackTok?

**A:**
ChackTok is built for real-world event operations where speed, stability, and repeatability matter.

* **Commercial workflow:** Event templates, structured settings, and repeatable setups for high-turnover events
* **Hardware ecosystem support:** Designed to work with a wide range of photo booth setups (subject to model/firmware)
* **Content customization:** Overlays, frames, stickers, text, music, and branded intro/outro (preroll/postroll)
* **Flexible capture modes:** Photo, video, GIF, and speed/effect modes depending on device capability
* **Guest-friendly delivery:** QR-based access and sharing options that reduce queues and operator workload

## Q5: How do I connect and control a 360 Photo Booth?

**A:**

1. **Enable Bluetooth** on your phone/tablet.
2. Open **ChackTok** → tap the **Connection** icon (top-left on the home screen).
3. Select your 360 booth from the device list to connect.
4. Go to the **Controller** page to adjust rotation settings (speed/direction/duration).
5. You can also set these parameters in **Event Edit** (where available).

## Q6: Why can’t I connect to my 360 Photo Booth?

**A:**
Common causes and fixes:

* **Compatibility:** Your booth model/firmware may not support app control.
* **Already connected elsewhere:** Disconnect the booth from other phones/tablets and try again.
* **Permissions:** Ensure ChackTok has Bluetooth permissions (and Location permission on some Android devices).
* **Restart:** Power-cycle the booth and restart the app.
* **Fallback option:** If Bluetooth control isn’t available, you can still use ChackTok for recording and event workflow. In many setups you can enable **Motion Trigger** to start recording when rotation is detected.

**Supported 360 booth brands (may vary by model/firmware):**
MWE, OLYLO, YCKJNB, ZANOGI, FUTOBOOZ, HARZHI, ZLPOWER, DIANDIAN, LIKVIDIU, JIYANG, KRICIKR

## Q9: Why can’t I connect to my iPad Photo Booth device?

**A:**
Try the following:

* Confirm **Bluetooth is enabled** and the device is not connected to another controller.
* Confirm the hardware is powered on (some control boxes show a status indicator light).

If the issue persists, contact **[support@chacktok.com](mailto:support@chacktok.com)**.

## Q10: How do I connect and use a GoPro (wired and wireless)?

**A:**

### 1) Wired connection

1. Connect GoPro to your phone/tablet using a **data-capable** USB cable.
2. In ChackTok: **Event Edit Page 2 → Camera Settings → Camera → GoPro**
3. ChackTok should detect the GoPro automatically.

### 2) Wireless connection (Wi-Fi)

1. Connect GoPro and your phone/tablet to the **same Wi-Fi network** (or use GoPro’s Wi-Fi where supported).
2. Put GoPro into pairing mode and enable Wi-Fi.
3. In ChackTok: **Event Edit Page 2 → Camera Settings → Camera → GoPro**
4. Search and connect.

---

## Q11: Why can’t I connect my GoPro?

**A:**

* Ensure ChackTok has **Bluetooth and Wi-Fi** permissions.
* Confirm GoPro is in **pairing mode**.
* For wireless: confirm both devices are on the **same network**.
* For wired: confirm the cable supports **data transfer** (not charging-only).
* Confirm your model is supported.

---

## Q12: GoPro file transfer is too slow. What can I do?

**A:**

* Lower GoPro **resolution/frame rate** to reduce file size.
* Improve Wi-Fi stability (use a stronger router or reduce network congestion).
* Prefer **wired** connection for faster transfer where possible.

---

## Q13: GoPro freezes/crashes/errors while using it with ChackTok

**A:**

* Update GoPro firmware.
* Lower recording settings (resolution/frame rate).
* Ensure battery and storage are sufficient.
* Prefer wired connection for stability.

If it persists, contact GoPro support and/or **[support@chacktok.com](mailto:support@chacktok.com)**.

## Q15: How do I cast to an external screen (extended vs mirroring)?

**A:**

* **Wireless:** Ensure your device and TV/monitor are on the same Wi-Fi network, then use system screen mirroring.
* **Wired:** Use HDMI/USB-C to HDMI adapter if supported.

**Casting modes (where available):**

* **Extended Screencasting:** Shows additional event content on the external screen.

  * Path: **Event Edit Page 4 → Extended Settings → Extended Screencasting**
* **Mirroring Screencasting:** Mirrors your main device screen.

  * Path: **Event Edit Page 4 → Extended Settings → Mirror Screencasting**
