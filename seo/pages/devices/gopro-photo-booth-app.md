# GoPro Photo Booth App Workflows

ChackTok supports GoPro photo booth use cases where the camera model, firmware, cable or wireless mode, mobile device, and app version are compatible.

## What Operators Should Test

GoPro workflows can improve capture quality for some 360 and event-video setups, but they add more event-day variables. Before a paid event, operators should test battery life, storage, firmware, connection mode, recording settings, file transfer, processing time, sharing output, and backup capture options.

## Setup Steps

1. Charge the GoPro and prepare storage.
2. Confirm firmware and selected recording settings.
3. Prepare the phone or tablet running ChackTok.
4. Connect the GoPro through the supported wired or wireless workflow.
5. Run a full test capture, process the output, and share it through the guest workflow.

## FAQ

### Which GoPro models should operators verify?

Public ChackTok documentation references supported GoPro workflows such as GoPro HERO 9-13 where available. Operators should confirm the current app version and exact model before a commercial event.

### Is GoPro required?

No. ChackTok can support different capture workflows depending on the booth, mobile device, app version, and event setup.
