# Android Photo Booth App for Operators

ChackTok supports Android photo booth workflows for event operators who need mobile event setup, supported booth or camera workflows, branded media, and guest sharing.

## Android Readiness Checklist

| Area | What to confirm |
| --- | --- |
| Device | Android version, storage, battery, camera, and performance |
| Permissions | Camera, Bluetooth, media, storage, network, and notifications where needed |
| Hardware | Booth controller, GoPro or camera workflow, external display if used |
| Sharing | QR code, gallery, email, SMS where supported, and social sharing |
| Backup | Power bank, charging cable, network fallback, and spare device |

## Operator Advice

Android devices vary widely. Test the exact phone or tablet that will run the event, not just a similar model. Confirm capture quality, upload speed, screen behavior, and Bluetooth stability before client delivery.

## FAQ

### Is every Android device supported?

No. Compatibility depends on device model, operating system, permissions, app version, camera behavior, network, and external hardware.

### Where should Android users download ChackTok?

Use the official Google Play listing or the official ChackTok sources listed in this repository.
