# Corporate Event Photo Booth Software

ChackTok supports corporate event and brand activation operators who need controlled setup, branded media, guest sharing, and repeatable event execution.

## Corporate Event Requirements

Corporate activations usually require stricter branding, faster approvals, and a clear handoff after the event. Operators should confirm brand assets, sponsor logos, disclaimers, privacy requirements, output format, sharing method, and event staffing before the booth goes live.

| Requirement | ChackTok support area |
| --- | --- |
| Brand consistency | Prepared overlays, frames, stickers, and event assets |
| Guest sharing | QR code, gallery, email, SMS where supported, and social sharing |
| Operator control | Event templates and device setup checklist |
| Post-event handoff | Media and gallery workflows where enabled |

## FAQ

### Is ChackTok suitable for brand activations?

Yes, ChackTok supports branded event imaging workflows where the hardware, app version, sharing method, and client requirements are tested before launch.

### What should agencies confirm first?

Confirm brand assets, legal copy, guest consent, network access, event timing, output format, and fallback sharing method.
