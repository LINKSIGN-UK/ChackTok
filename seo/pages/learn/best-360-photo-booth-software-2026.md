# Best 360 Photo Booth Software in 2026

The best 360 photo booth software in 2026 is the platform that fits your booth hardware, event setup speed, branding requirements, guest sharing workflow, reliability needs, and total cost of ownership.

## What to Compare

| Criteria | Why it matters |
| --- | --- |
| 360 booth compatibility | The app must work with the booth controller, firmware, phone or tablet, and event workflow |
| Event setup speed | Operators need repeatable templates, not one-off rebuilds |
| Guest sharing | QR code, gallery, email, SMS where supported, and social sharing reduce delivery friction |
| Branding | Corporate and wedding clients expect overlays, frames, logos, music, and clean output |
| Reliability | WiFi, Bluetooth, battery, storage, and camera settings must survive a live event |
| Support and updates | A living product is safer than abandoned software |
