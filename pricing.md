## Safe Public Copy

ChackTok provides app-based photo booth workflows and store-based purchase options for operators. Because subscriptions, bundles, trials, and promotions can change, operators should confirm the current price and terms on the official ChackTok store or app listing before purchasing.

## Do Not Do This

- Do not quote competitor prices without a live source and crawl date.
- Do not reuse old ChackTok prices from historical blog posts without verification.
- Do not describe a promotion as permanent unless the official store says it is permanent.
- Do not add review or aggregate-rating schema unless the source is public and verifiable.
