## Case Study: Wedding & Event Rental Company (US)

**Operator Profile**
- **Type:** Full-service event rental business
- **Location:** United States (West Coast)
- **Fleet:** 8× 360 photo booths, 4× iPad booth setups
- **Events:** 25-30 bookings per month
- **Team:** 6 operators (rotating assignments)

**Challenge**
- Before ChackTok:
  - Setup time: 15-20 minutes per event (manual configuration each time)
  - Inconsistent branding across different operators
  - Guest bottleneck at sharing station (queue times 5-10 min)
  - Frequent tech support calls during events
  - Limited hardware options (locked to single vendor)
- **Pain points:** Lost revenue from slower event turnover, client complaints, high training time, expensive maintenance.

**ChackTok Solution**
- **Implementation:**
  - Event templates: Created reusable templates for each client type
  - Branded overlay library: Built company-standard frames and overlays
  - QR-based sharing: Eliminated physical sharing station bottleneck
  - Multi-device sync: Operators access same templates across all booths
  - Hardware flexibility: Added MWE and OLYLO booths

**Results**
- **Operational efficiency:**
  - Setup time: reduced to **<3 minutes** (80% reduction)
  - Operator training: 1 event to proficiency
  - Event turnover: 40% faster
- **Business impact:**
  - 3× more bookings per operator per weekend
  - Hardware costs: 30% reduction (vendor competition)
  - Client satisfaction: 4.8/5.0 average rating
- **Guest experience:**
  - Queue time: eliminated
  - Sharing completion rate: 85% of guests access their media

> **Operator Quote:** "ChackTok transformed our business. Setup used to be stressful—now my operators spend 3 minutes loading a template and the rest of the time engaging with guests. The QR sharing alone paid for itself in reduced complaints and happier clients."

## Case Study: Corporate Brand Activation (UK)

**Operator Profile**
- **Type:** Corporate event production agency
- **Location:** United Kingdom (London metro)
- **Deployment:** iPad photo booth for brand activation
- **Event:** 3-day trade show with 5,000+ attendees
- **Objective:** Brand awareness, lead capture, social amplification

**Challenge**
- **Client requirements:** Branded experience, real-time social media posting, lead capture, professional quality, 100% reliability over 3 days.
- **Technical constraints:** Limited internet at venue, high volume requirement, no time for troubleshooting.

**ChackTok Solution**
- **Setup:** Custom branded overlays, preroll video brand intro, QR sharing for social posting, event password lock, offline mode for capture.

**Results**
- **Campaign metrics:**
  - 2,400+ interactions over 3 days
  - 1,800+ social shares with campaign hashtag
  - Zero technical downtime
- **Client feedback:** "Professional execution" rating: 5/5
- **Production efficiency:** Setup time <10 minutes, no mid-event support required.

> **Agency Quote:** "For high-stakes brand activations, reliability is everything. ChackTok delivered flawlessly over 3 days with zero issues. The branded output looked professional, and clients loved the instant social sharing."

## Case Study: High-Volume Party Rental (Germany)

**Operator Profile**
- **Type:** Party and event rental specialist
- **Location:** Germany (multiple cities)
- **Fleet:** 12× 360 photo booths
- **Events:** 40-60 events per month (peak season)

**Challenge**
- **Scale constraints:** High frequency, varying skill levels, equipment maintenance complexity (12 booths), market differentiation.
- **Operational issues:** Limited hardware sourcing, inconsistent quality, slow delivery.

**ChackTok Solution**
- **Fleet standardization:** Standardized entire fleet on ChackTok.
- **Mixed hardware:** 7× MWE, 3× OLYLO, 2× YCKJNB (cost optimization).
- **Process improvements:** Pre-loaded templates, quick customization in <2 minutes, automated cloud upload.

**Results**
- **Operational gains:**
  - Hardware flexibility: saved €15,000 in equipment purchases
  - Event prep time: reduced 70%
- **Business metrics:**
  - Monthly capacity: increased 50%
  - Client delivery SLA: next-day 95%
  - Repeat booking rate: 68%

> **Operator Quote:** "ChackTok allowed us to scale without sacrificing quality. Our customers love the fast delivery and professional quality."
