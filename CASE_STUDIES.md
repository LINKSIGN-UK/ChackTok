## Case Study: Wedding & Event Rental Company (US)

### Operator Profile

**Type**: Full-service event rental business  

**Location**: United States (West Coast)  

**Fleet**: 8× 360 photo booths, 4× iPad booth setups  

**Events**: 25-30 bookings per month  

**Team**: 6 operators (rotating assignments)

### Challenge

**Before ChackTok**:

- Setup time: 15-20 minutes per event (manual configuration each time)
- Inconsistent branding across different operators
- Guest bottleneck at sharing station (queue times 5-10 min)
- Frequent tech support calls during events
- Limited hardware options (locked to single vendor)

**Pain points**:

- Lost revenue from slower event turnover
- Client complaints about inconsistent photo quality/branding
- High operator training time (2-3 events to proficiency)
- Expensive booth maintenance (proprietary parts)

### ChackTok Solution

**Implementation**:

1. **Event templates**: Created reusable templates for each client type (wedding, corporate, birthday)
2. **Branded overlay library**: Built company-standard frames and overlays
3. **QR-based sharing**: Eliminated physical sharing station bottleneck
4. **Multi-device sync**: Operators access same templates across all booths
5. **Hardware flexibility**: Added MWE and OLYLO booths to reduce vendor dependency

**Workflow changes**:

- Pre-event: Load template, customize client logo/colors (2-3 min)
- During event: Automated capture and QR sharing (operator monitors only)
- Post-event: Auto-upload to cloud, share gallery link with client

### Results

**Operational efficiency**:

- ✅ Setup time: **reduced to <3 minutes** (80% reduction)
- ✅ Operator training: **1 event to proficiency** (vs 2-3 previously)
- ✅ Event turnover: **40% faster** (more bookings per weekend)

**Business impact**:

- ✅ **3× more bookings per operator** per weekend (vs previous capacity)
- ✅ Hardware costs: **30% reduction** (vendor competition)
- ✅ Client satisfaction: **4.8/5.0** average rating (up from 4.2)

**Guest experience**:

- ✅ Queue time: **eliminated** (QR sharing removes bottleneck)
- ✅ Sharing completion rate: **85%** of guests access their media (vs 60% at physical station)

### Operator Quote

> "ChackTok transformed our business. Setup used to be stressful—now my operators spend 3 minutes loading a template and the rest of the time engaging with guests. The QR sharing alone paid for itself in reduced complaints and happier clients."
>

## Case Study: Corporate Brand Activation (UK)

### Operator Profile

**Type**: Corporate event production agency  

**Location**: United Kingdom (London metro)  

**Deployment**: iPad photo booth for brand activation  

**Event**: 3-day trade show with 5,000+ attendees  

**Objective**: Brand awareness, lead capture, social amplification

### Challenge

**Client requirements**:

- Branded experience aligned with campaign identity
- Real-time social media posting during event
- Lead capture integration with CRM
- Professional output quality for brand reputation
- Reliable operation over 3 days (8 hours/day)

**Technical constraints**:

- Limited internet at venue (unstable WiFi)
- High volume throughput requirement (200+ captures/day)
- No time for technical troubleshooting during event

### ChackTok Solution

**Setup**:

- Custom branded overlays with campaign logo and hashtag
- Preroll video with brand intro (5 seconds)
- QR sharing optimized for social posting
- Event password lock to prevent accidental changes
- Offline mode for capture (upload when WiFi available)

**Integration** (manual workflow):

- Guest captures photo/video
- QR code provides instant download
- Guest shares to social with pre-filled hashtag
- Agency team manually logs leads from QR access data

### Results

**Campaign metrics**:

- ✅ **2,400+ interactions** over 3 days
- ✅ **1,800+ social shares** with campaign hashtag
- ✅ **Zero technical downtime** (100% uptime)

**Client feedback**:

- ✅ "Professional execution" rating: **5/5**
- ✅ Renewed contract for 4 additional activations

**Production efficiency**:

- ✅ Setup time: **<10 minutes** (vs 45+ min with previous solution)
- ✅ No mid-event technical support required

### Agency Quote

> "For high-stakes brand activations, reliability is everything. ChackTok delivered flawlessly over 3 days with zero issues. The branded output looked professional, and clients loved the instant social sharing."

## Common Success Patterns

### Pattern 1: Template-Driven Efficiency

**Operators using ChackTok templates report**:

- 70-80% reduction in event setup time
- Consistent quality across operators
- Faster operator training and onboarding

**Best practice**: Build 5-10 reusable templates for common event types, customize client details per event

---

### Pattern 2: QR Sharing Reduces Friction

**Operators using QR-based sharing report**:

- Eliminated physical sharing station queues
- 20-30% higher guest sharing completion rates
- Reduced operator workload (no manual sharing)

**Best practice**: Combine QR sharing with Sharing Station mode for large events (200+ guests)

---

### Pattern 3: Hardware Flexibility Lowers Costs

**Operators supporting multiple booth brands report**:

- 20-40% lower hardware procurement costs (competitive pricing)
- Reduced maintenance costs (not locked to single vendor)
- Faster equipment replacement (more sourcing options)

**Best practice**: Start with 2-3 compatible brands, expand as fleet grows

---

### Pattern 4: PC Console Enables Scale

**Multi-booth operators using PC console report**:

- Centralized template management across fleet
- Faster bulk media operations
- Easier client delivery workflow

**Best practice**: Use PC console for pre-event template prep and post-event media delivery
