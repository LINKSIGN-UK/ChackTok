## Quick Workflow: First Event

Use this as a compact event-day checklist:

1. Install ChackTok from the official App Store or Google Play listing.
2. Register or log in.
3. Confirm VIP, account, or device binding status if your workflow requires it.
4. Create a new event or choose a template.
5. Add event name, overlay, frame, music, sharing settings, and gallery options.
6. Connect hardware if needed.
7. Capture a short test media item.
8. Verify the media saves correctly.
9. Test QR code, gallery, email, SMS, or social sharing.
10. Keep a backup device, power source, and network plan ready before guests arrive.
