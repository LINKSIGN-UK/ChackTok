# Device Binding and VIP Usage

This guide explains the public-facing Device Binding and VIP workflow at a practical level. Plan availability, benefits, and labels may vary by region, account, and app version.

## What Device Binding Is

Device Binding links eligible equipment or a device number to a ChackTok account or usage entitlement workflow. It is commonly used when operators need to activate supported free usage time or related device benefits.

## What to Prepare

- ChackTok account.
- Device number or eligible equipment information.
- Proof of purchase or order information if support asks for it.
- Stable network connection.
- Latest app version.

## Binding Workflow

1. Open ChackTok and log in.
2. Find the Device Binding entry in the app.
3. Enter or scan the required device number.
4. Confirm the account and device information.
5. Submit the binding request.
6. Check whether the app shows updated usage time, VIP status, or entitlement status.
7. Restart the app if the status does not refresh immediately.

## Free Usage Time

Some eligible devices or bundles may include free usage time. If available, it is usually activated through Device Binding or an account entitlement workflow.

If free usage time does not appear:

- Confirm that the device is eligible.
- Check whether the device number was entered correctly.
- Confirm that the account is the intended account.
- Check network connection.
- Contact support with the account, device number, and purchase details.

## VIP Notes

VIP can affect features such as watermark behavior, event functions, sharing tools, or commercial usage capabilities depending on current plan rules. Always check the current in-app information for exact plan details.

## Common Issues

| Issue | Action |
| --- | --- |
| Device number rejected | Check spelling, number format, and eligibility |
| Status not refreshed | Restart app and reconnect network |
| Bound to wrong account | Contact support with details |
| VIP benefit unclear | Check in-app plan page or contact support |
